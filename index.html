Oz Foundation

About Us

Oz Foundation has been helping scholars since 2004. We are a family foundation that strongly believes a college education is the gateway to a successful future. We feel that financial hardship should not deter individuals from reaching this goal.

Oz Foundation is a family foundation that operates in Santa Clara and San Diego Counties. We feel strongly about the value of a college education, and help to provide for those who would otherwise not be able to afford it. In addition to financial support, we provide personal mentorship to assist our students in their studies and the adjustment to college life.

Prospective Scholars

Criteria:

  • You must be a high school senior or junior college transfer student.
  • You must have attended high school in Santa Clara County or San Diego County.
  • This program is for low-income families. If accepted, you will need to provide supporting documentation such as tax returns.
  • You must have already been accepted to a four-year accredited university.

All applicants will be notified by mail or telephone regarding acceptance or decline. Award amounts are based on individual need.

What to Expect if Accepted

As long as you maintain your grades and are enrolled as a full-time student (with supporting paperwork), you will be part of our program for up to four years. You will also be assigned a mentor to communicate directly with. We generally accept 2-5 new students per year.

Current Scholars

As a current scholar, you don’t receive a one-time gift; we support you throughout your four years at the university.
